How it works

From raw prospect data to booked meetings in four steps.

Connect your data sources

Link your Shopify store, CRM, or upload a prospect list. Our system ingests product catalogs, customer segments, and behavioral data to build rich prospect profiles.

AI scores and qualifies

Our fine-tuned NLP models analyze each prospect across 40+ signals: website content, social presence, tech stack, growth indicators, and buying patterns. Only prospects with confidence >0.7 proceed.

Personalized sequences generated

AWS Bedrock generates unique, context-aware email sequences for each qualified prospect. Every message references specific details about their business — no generic outreach.

Send, track, optimize

Emails are delivered via Amazon SES with optimal timing. Responses are classified automatically. Positive replies surface instantly. The system learns from every interaction to improve over time.

Built on enterprise infrastructure

We use AWS services for reliability, security, and scale. No vendor lock-in — standard protocols, exportable data.

AWS Bedrock (Claude, Titan) Amazon SES AWS Lambda DynamoDB Python 3.12 PostgreSQL EventBridge CloudWatch

When we started building our inference pipeline, we evaluated self-hosting open models vs. managed services. The math was clear: at our current scale (sub-1M tokens/day), Bedrock's pay-per-use model costs 70% less than maintaining GPU instances, with better latency and zero ops overhead.
