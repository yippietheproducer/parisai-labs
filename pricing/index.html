FAQ

Are AWS Bedrock token costs included?

Yes. The plan price covers all model inference costs. We absorb token volatility because we use a Haiku-first / Sonnet-on-the-margin pipeline that keeps marginal cost predictable.

What if I exceed my monthly send quota?

We pause sends and notify you. No automatic overage billing. You can either upgrade or roll the unsent volume into next month.

Where is data stored?

EU-West-1 (Ireland) by default. US-East-1 available on the Scale plan. All data is encrypted at rest with KMS and in transit with TLS 1.3.

Do you sign a DPA?

Yes, on Growth and Scale plans. Standard EU-GDPR compliant DPA with optional SCCs for non-EU data flows.
